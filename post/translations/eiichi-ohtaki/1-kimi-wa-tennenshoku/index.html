「君は天然色」 大滝詠一 (1981)

2020-09-18 493 words 1 min read

Contents

The title of the song translates to You are Technicolored (alludes to the finnicky nature of the woman/girl being referred in the song) by Eiichi Ohtaki from his 1981 album A Long Vacation. The song is at No.1 on Side A of the album.

Note: A Long Vacation is one of my favorite Japanese Albums. I wanted to translate this for a long time but never really got around it until mid of last month when I finally decided to buckle up and start translating it right from the first song.

くちびるつんと尖らせて 何かたくらむ表情は
Pouting your lips with that scheming face
別れの気配を ポケットに匿していたから
Because you had the token of breakup in your pockets
机の端の ポラロイド写真に話しかけてたら
When I tried talking to the polaroid at the edge of the table
過ぎ去った過去 しゃくだけど今より眩しい
The past that has gone by seems like an illusion even though it was so painful

想い出は モノクローム 色を点けてくれ
My memories are in monochrome please color them
もう一度 そばに来て
Come to me once more
はなやいで うるわしの Color Girl
Cheerful and beautiful Color Girl

夜明けまで長電話して 受話器持つ手がしびれたね
耳もとに触れたささやきは 今も忘れない

想い出は モノクローム 色を点けてくれ

渚を滑るディンキーで 手を振る君の小指から
流れ出す虹の幻で 空を染めてくれ
